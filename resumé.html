Professional Summary

Lucas Campbell is a recent graduate from the University of Calgary’s School of Architecture, Planning, and Landscape—Canada’s premier design-focused planning school—with a Master’s Degree in Planning. He is interested in dense housing, walkable communities, active and public transportation, and climate change solutions enabled by both policy and design. He has worked professionally as a Planning Assistant with the City of Regina during the Summer of 2024.

Previously, Lucas completed a Bachelor’s of Arts Degree in Economics & Society, minoring in both Computer Science and Geography & Environmental Studies at the University of Regina before working as a Business Metrics Analyst.
